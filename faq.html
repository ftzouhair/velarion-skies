Common Questions & Answers

We provide access to a wide range of aircraft, from light jets for short hops to ultra-long-range jets for international travel, as well as helicopters. Each operator is vetted for safety and service to meet the highest standards.

We can often arrange flights within 4–6 hours depending on availability. However, for best aircraft selection and pricing, we recommend booking as far in advance as possible, especially during peak travel seasons.

Yes, pets are accepted on most routes — please notify us at booking so we can arrange crew and cabin requirements to ensure a comfortable and safe journey for your animal companions.

We exclusively partner with operators audited to ARGUS Platinum, Wyvern, and IS-BAO standards. All aircraft undergo recurrent crew training, maintenance checks, and third-party audits to ensure your safety is never compromised.

Yes, we provide bespoke in-flight catering with options from light refreshments to multi-course meals. Our culinary team works with top chefs to create personalized menus tailored to your tastes and dietary requirements.

Changes can be made up to 48 hours before departure without penalty. Within 48 hours, changes may incur fees depending on availability. Our concierge team will assist with all modifications to ensure a seamless experience.

Cancellations made 48+ hours before departure receive a full refund. Within 48 hours, 50% refund is provided. For multi-leg or group bookings, special terms may apply and will be outlined in your contract.

Yes, we arrange luxury ground transportation to and from the airport with premium vehicles and professional chauffeurs. This service can be added to your booking for a seamless door-to-door experience.
